Privacy Policy for MapChat

Last updated: May 2025

1. Introduction

MapChat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Username, email address (if provided for support), and password (stored in hashed form).
Location Data: Your device's GPS location is collected to provide core app functionality.
Usage Data: We collect anonymized information about your interactions with the app for analytics and improvement.
Media: If you share photos, audio, or text messages in chats, these are stored securely on our server.
3. How We Use Your Information

To provide and maintain MapChat functionality
To communicate with users (e.g., support, notifications)
To improve and optimize our app and services
To comply with legal obligations
4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.
Data may be shared with law enforcement if required by law.
Group chat content is visible to group participants.
5. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure authentication. However, no system is 100% secure.

You may update or correct your information in the app.
